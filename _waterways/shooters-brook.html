The lower (beyond Piccadilly Station) part of the route was plotted with a map from this thread on the Canalworld Discussion Forum, some from the six inch 1845 Ordnance Survey map and the rest from The Lost Rivers of Manchester, the map from which is reproduced online in A deeper understanding of climate induced risk to urban infrastructure: case studies of past events in Greater Manchester.
